Rapids! Make Memories!

At River Rush Rafting our mission is to provide thrilling, safe, and memorable river adventures for families, groups, and thrill-seekers. Our experienced guides, top-quality equipment, and commitment to safety make every trip unforgettable.

History

Founded in 2010 by a team of passionate river guides, River Rush began as small weekend trips run by a close group of friends who shared a love for adventure and the outdoors. These early trips focused on creating fun, safe, and memorable experiences on the river, fostering a sense of camaraderie and exploration among participants.

Over the years, River Rush has grown to offer a wide range of adventures, including one-day trips, immersive multi-day expeditions, and custom group experiences tailored to all skill levels. Our experienced guides, top-quality equipment, and dedication to safety ensure that every journey is thrilling yet secure. We strive to inspire a love of the river, create lasting memories, and build a community of adventurers who share our passion for the outdoors.
